Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our home cleaning services

How often should I have my home cleaned?

The frequency depends on your lifestyle, family size, and personal preferences. Most clients choose weekly or bi-weekly cleaning for optimal maintenance.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you don't need to be present. Many of our clients provide access and go about their day while we clean. We're fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind.

What cleaning products do you use?

We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for children and pets. We can also accommodate specific product preferences upon request.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you prefer us to use your specific products, we're happy to accommodate that request.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?

We guarantee your satisfaction. If you're not completely happy with our service, we'll return within 24 hours to re-clean any areas of concern at no additional charge.

How do you handle fragile items?

Our team is trained to handle delicate items with care. We recommend securing very valuable or extremely fragile items before our arrival.
