About Sweepo

New Zealand's Most Trusted Cleaning Service

Since our founding, Sweepo has been dedicated to providing exceptional cleaning services across New Zealand. We believe that a clean environment leads to a happier, healthier, and more productive life.

Our Story

Sweepo was founded with a simple mission: to give people more time to focus on what matters most by taking care of their cleaning needs. What started as a small local cleaning service has grown into New Zealand's most trusted cleaning company.

Our founder recognized that in today's busy world, people need reliable partners who can maintain their homes and workplaces to the highest standards. This vision led to the creation of Sweepo - where professional cleaning meets exceptional service.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers across New Zealand, from residential homes to large commercial facilities. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction remains as strong as ever.
